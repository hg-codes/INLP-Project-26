Each team must submit a 8-10 page document (excluding references) that includes the
project idea and 
motivation/objectives, 
background and related work (with references to relevant and updated papers), 
dataset details, 
proposed methodology/architecture and 
evaluation options. 
Also include the progress so far (objectives completed, ongoing, and planned) and 
tentative timeline.

The purpose of this document is to provide a clear picture of your project’s direction and current progress.

The report must follow the ACL Style Template, which is available here.

Additionally, please either- make your GitHub repository public and share
the link, or share access privately with your mentor and co-mentors.

Submit as a PDF with the filename: <TeamName>-Mid.pdf. One submission per team.

You will be graded on the code, report and a viva. 
Having slides for the viva is optional
